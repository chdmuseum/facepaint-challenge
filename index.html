#FacePaintChallenge

organized on the occasion of

World Environment Day
5th June 2020

Greetings,

During the week of 23rd May,2020 to 3rd June,2020, Government Museum and Art Gallery, Chandigarh invited entries for a digital #FacePaintChallenge through its social media channels to stimulate conversations on the theme of "Biodiversity" for the World Environment Day, celebrated on 5th June, 2020.

We extend our thanks to everyone who participated in the challenge and helped us spread their concerns regarding the changing patterns of nature - human dynamic.


THANK YOU


We have showcased selected entries in the Gallery along with attribution to artist.
